Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a medicine?

Tap the + button on the home screen, fill in the medicine details (name, dosage, quantity, expiry date), and tap Save. You can also attach a photo of the medicine box.

How do I add a patient profile?

Go to the Patients section and tap Add Patient. Once created, you can assign medicines to each patient profile separately.

Why am I not receiving expiry alerts?

  • Make sure notifications are enabled: go to iPhone Settings → CarerMeds → Notifications and turn them on.
  • Check that the medicine has an expiry date entered correctly.
  • Alerts are sent 30 days before expiry by default.

Can I use CarerMeds on multiple devices?

Yes. Sign in with the same account on any iPhone or iPad and your data will sync automatically via Firebase.

How do I delete a medicine or prescription?

Swipe left on the item in the list and tap Delete, or open the item and use the delete option in the top-right menu.

How do I delete my account and all my data?

Email us at pradeepnswamy@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and we will permanently remove your account and all associated data within 30 days.

Is my health data private?

Yes. Your data is stored in your private Firebase account and is never shared with third parties or used for advertising. Photos are stored locally on your device only. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Troubleshooting

The app is crashing or not loading

  • Force-close the app and reopen it.
  • Check you have a stable internet connection for initial sign-in.
  • Make sure your iOS is up to date.
  • Delete and reinstall the app — your data is safely stored in the cloud and will restore on sign-in.

My data is not syncing

  • Check your internet connection.
  • Sign out and sign back in from the app's Settings screen.
  • If the issue persists, contact us with your email address and we'll investigate.
